About FeedForward

FeedForward is a web platform that connects restaurants with excess food to people or NGOs in need. Our goal is to reduce food wastage and promote community welfare.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable ecosystem where surplus food reaches those in need, minimizing environmental impact and enhancing social good.
