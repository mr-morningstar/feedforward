About Us

FeedForward is a web platform that connects restaurants with excess food to people or NGOs in need. Our aim is to reduce food wastage and promote community welfare through seamless and transparent distribution of surplus meals.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable ecosystem that prevents food from going to waste and delivers it to those who need it most. We believe in collaboration, compassion, and community-driven action to eliminate hunger one meal at a time.
